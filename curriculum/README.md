# Curriculum Alignment & OBE Matrix
This module is built on **Constructive Alignment**, ensuring learning activities directly support professional outcomes.

## 1. The OBE Trio
* **PEO (Program Educational Objectives)**: Developing ethical leaders in AI integration.
* **PLO (Program Learning Outcomes)**: Competence in digital literacy, critical thinking, and ethical AI usage.
* **CLO (Course Learning Outcomes)**: Analyzing physiological disorders and evaluating AI claim accuracy.

## 2. Course Design Matrix (CDM)
| Learning Outcome | Activity (AI Integration) | Assessment Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Analyze Disorders** | AI as Research Assistant for molecular causes. | Summative Post-test & Mastery metrics. |
| **Digital Literacy** | **Critical Step**: Cross-referencing AI claims with textbooks. | Digital Literacy Rubric (Evaluation skills). |
| **Creative Intervention** | AI as Brainstorming Partner for health campaigns. | Product Evaluation (PSA Script/Poster). |
